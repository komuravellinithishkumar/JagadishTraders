## User Personas
1. **Contractors & Builders**: Looking for reliable cement and construction materials supplier
2. **Engineers**: Seeking authorized cement dealers for projects
3. **Real Estate Developers**: Need bulk construction materials
4. **Individual House Builders**: Looking for trusted local supplier

## Core Requirements (Static)
- Professional, trustworthy design reflecting 30+ years of legacy
- SEO-optimized for local search (Karimnagar, Telangana)
- Display all 8 authorized cement brands
- Showcase complete product range
- Contact information with Call & WhatsApp buttons
- Google Maps integration
- Mobile-responsive design
- Fast loading performance

## What's Been Implemented (Date: Feb 1, 2025)

### Frontend - COMPLETED ✅
- **Hero Section**: High-impact design with construction imagery, "ESTABLISHED 1995" badge, Call Now & WhatsApp buttons
- **About Section**: Trust-building content highlighting 30+ years of service, family-run business details
- **Why Choose Us Section**: Three key value propositions with icons (Trusted Since 1995, Authorized Dealer, Wide Product Range)
- **Cement Brands Section**: Grid display of 8 authorized cement brands (UltraTech, Ambuja, Chettinad, Maha, ACC, Sagar, MPL, Dalmia)
- **Products Section**: Visual showcase with 3 category cards + comprehensive product list (17 categories)
- **Contact Section**: Full contact details, phone numbers with click-to-call, WhatsApp integration, Google Maps embed
- **Footer**: Business information and copyright

### Design System
- Following Pixel Pushers design guidelines
- Color Scheme: Dark backgrounds (#1a1c1b), Lime green accents (#d9fb06), Olive tones for cards
- Typography: PP Right Grotesk for headings, Inter for body text
- Pill-shaped buttons with hover animations
- Professional construction industry imagery

### SEO Implementation
- Structured data (LocalBusiness schema) with all business details
- Meta tags optimized for local search
- Keywords: "cement dealer Karimnagar", "construction materials Karimnagar", "hardware shop Karimnagar"
- Open Graph tags for social sharing

### Technical Stack
- Frontend: React with Shadcn UI components
- Styling: Tailwind CSS with custom design system
- Images: Professional stock images (placeholders for actual store photos)
